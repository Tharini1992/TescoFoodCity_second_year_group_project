Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our delivery service

How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes between 24-72 hours depending on your location. Express delivery (same-day) is available in Colombo and surrounding areas for orders placed before 12 PM.
Can I track my order?
Yes! You can track your order in real-time using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Simply enter it in the tracking box above to see your order status.
Do you deliver outside major cities?
Yes, we deliver to suburban and rural areas across Sri Lanka. Delivery times may vary based on location. Contact us for specific area availability.
What are the delivery charges?
Delivery is FREE for orders above Rs. 3,000! For orders below that, a flat delivery fee of Rs. 250-500 applies depending on your location.
What if I'm not home during delivery?
Our delivery partner will contact you before arrival. If you're unavailable, we can leave the order with a trusted neighbor or reschedule for a convenient time.
