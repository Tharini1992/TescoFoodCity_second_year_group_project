Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes between 24-72 hours depending on your location. Express delivery is available in Colombo.
Can I track my order?
Yes! Enter your tracking number above to see your order status in real time.
Do you deliver outside the city?
Yes, we deliver to suburban and rural areas across the country.
What are the delivery charges?
Delivery is FREE for orders above Rs. 3,000. Otherwise, Rs. 250-500 depending on location.
